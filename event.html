Vic Falls Carnival

November 18, 2026
Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Doors: 8:00 PM

About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable night at one of Southern Africa's most anticipated annual celebrations. The Vic Falls Carnival brings together music lovers from across the continent for a week-long celebration of music, art, and culture.

Zimbo Artist will be performing on the Main Stage, bringing the energy of the Echoes of Tomorrow World Tour to thousands of fans. Expect an electrifying set featuring hits from across our discography, stunning visuals, and a performance you won't forget.

Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Victoria Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, the carnival offers the perfect setting for a night of incredible music under the African stars.
